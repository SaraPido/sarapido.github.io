The Human Behind the Research

Technology should serve people and improve everyday life.

I'm Sara — an AI researcher who genuinely loves what I do. I spent my PhD making data science accessible to everyone, then took that energy to MIT, back to Milan, and into the mountains on weekends.

I'm driven by the belief that the best AI research happens when you stay close to people — their needs, their confusion, their delight. Whether I'm building explainability tools, mentoring a student, or optimizing a model for deployment on a tiny chip, the question I keep asking is: does this actually help someone?
